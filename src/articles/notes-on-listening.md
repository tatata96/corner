# Notes on Listening

Listening starts before the signal becomes clear.

The room, the device, and the body all decide what gets through. I am interested in the moments where that filtering becomes visible, where a message is shaped by the surface it passes through.

## Working Notes

- Keep the source close to the viewer.
- Let noise remain part of the structure.
- Treat silence as an active material.
